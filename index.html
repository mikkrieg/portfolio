About Me

Thanks for taking some time to learn more about me! I currently work at the Tualatin Country Club as a server, focusing primarily in hospitality and member service.

I studied at Portland Community College for four years. My first two years were very foggy and I had no clear goal which dimmed my motivation, causing me to fumble about aimlessly. Because of this I decided to try out coding through the college's Web Development & Design program. After taking my first term of classes my motivation was rekindled! I had discovered an interest and passion in coding! Primarily with building websites and watching them come together piece by piece.

Now that I have graduated from Portland Community College with an associates in web development & design. I have decided to further build upon my education with Epicodus coding Bootcamp. My studies at Portland Community College focused primarily on front end coding. My plan is to graduate Epicodus with improved front end skills and new strong back end skills. After completing the program I will pursue a career in software development!
